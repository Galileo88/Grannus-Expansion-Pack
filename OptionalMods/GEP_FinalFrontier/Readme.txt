## Final Frontier

1. Included here are the GEP-specific ribbons used by the mod Final Frontier. Final Frontier itself is not included.

2. To use, first download and install [Final Frontier](https://spacedock.info/mod/580/Final%20Frontier).

3. Copy/merge [GEP Download]\Optional Mods\GEP_FinalFrontier\GameData\Nereid to [KSP]\GameData\ .
