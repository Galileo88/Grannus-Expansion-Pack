## Final Frontier

Included here are the GEP-specific ribbons used by the mod Final Frontier. Final Frontier itself is not included and must be installed separately.

To install Final Frontier:

1. Download and install [Final Frontier](https://spacedock.info/mod/580/Final%20Frontier).

2. Copy/merge [GEP Download]\Optional Mods\GEP_FinalFrontier\GameData\Nereid to [KSP]\GameData\ .
